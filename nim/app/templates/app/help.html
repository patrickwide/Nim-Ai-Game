How To Play Nim

  1. Nim is two player game.

  2. In this case, that means you and the computer.

  3. When you start the game, four(4) piles consisting of an addition of two(2) stones from top.

  4. Both players take turns. Each turn a player must remove one or more stones from a pile.

  5. The player that takes the last stone(s) from the only remaining pile looses. Easy, eh?
